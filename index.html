Who we are

Chariot is a ride-sharing platform committed to transforming everyday transportation through innovation, reliability, and technology. Chariot connects passengers with trusted drivers in a seamless, efficient, and secure manner. Our mission is to provide a dependable alternative to traditional transport options, offering a technology-driven solution that enhances convenience while fostering economic opportunity. By partnering with local drivers and tailoring our services to urban and peri-urban environments, we aim to create a system that benefits both users and service providers.
